Our Projects!

We are aimed at attracting all students who have a passion to learn and engage in projects within the computer and technology fields. We will be especially open to beginners or first time users of computer science and programming, but will also be able to engage more advanced students. Our club will employ projects to teach skills and strategies that will be applicable to many fields, from computers to biology and chemistry. These projects may include a faculty member or outside expert from the respective field to further promote the usefulness of the skill being taught.
